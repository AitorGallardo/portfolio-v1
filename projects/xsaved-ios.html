XSaved for iOS https://xsaved.com

Published Jun 2026

Your X bookmarks, beautifully in your pocket.

The same bookmarks you've been saving on X, now in a fast, gorgeous app you can flick through anywhere — on the train, in line for coffee, whenever something comes to mind.

A feed worth scrolling

Your saves are laid out in a clean, image-first grid that feels right at home on iPhone. Photos, quotes, threads — all in one calm, browsable place.

Search that keeps up

Looking for something specific? Start typing and your whole library narrows down on the spot.

Every post, the full picture

Tap any bookmark to open it in full — the words, the media, who posted it — without bouncing back out to X.

Coming soon

XSaved for iPhone is in active development, built natively for iOS. The XSaved Chrome extension is live today at xsaved.com.
